Surfied

Surfied is the Serious Game that provides surf clubs with a more interactive and efficient way to collect and verify membership information and keep members actively engaged with events, news, media, like-minded people, and the exploration of ocean coasts.

View project onGitHub

Please download the source code from GitHub. And visit Surfied to login and play the Serious Game designed for Surf Clubs.
